Who is Tsunomaki Watame ?

Watame is fluffiness and wholesomeness personified into one cute Sheep package. Hardworking, humorous and a memelord, no one will ever catch her being boring.

Over the past year Watame has fostered a strong community through efforts to connect with her fans regardless of location and us Watamates would like to repay in kind.

Singing Sheep

Watame's primary field of operation is music, whether it be originals, covers or her many weekly music streams.

She sets goals to produce more original songs, her own original album and aiming for a solo live concert. She fully intends to do this while continuing to stream content, truly a determined Sheep.
